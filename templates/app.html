Privacy notice

Store only project-relevant material. Students may use first names or pseudonyms, and this version stores work only inside the course-linked studio.

Academic integrity

AI suggestions are prompts for judgment, not final submissions. Students must revise, select, explain, and own the work they turn in.

Pedagogical stance

The studio is designed to lower cognitive load while increasing ethical reflection, coherence, and documentation of process.

Instructor review

Progress, unresolved questions, and AI-use reflections are visible in the instructor dashboard, but the tool does not assign grades.
